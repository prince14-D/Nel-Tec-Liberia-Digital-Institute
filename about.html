About Nel-Tec

We Train People to Lead in a Digital Future

Nel-Tec Liberia is committed to closing the skills gap through practical, affordable, and community-centered technology education.

Our Story

A Learning Hub Built for Real-Life Results

We started with one goal: to make quality digital education accessible to Liberians and Africans who are ready to build meaningful careers and businesses.

Our training model combines clear instruction, practical assignments, and community support to help learners gain confidence, master tools, and produce work that matters.
